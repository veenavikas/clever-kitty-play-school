Junior KG

At CleverKitty, our Junior KG program strengthens the academic foundation built in Nursery while enhancing communication skills, confidence, and overall personality development. We follow an ICSE-based curriculum, combining structured academics with creative and activity-based learning.

Join Junior KG
Core Subjects

Academic Program 📚

Our Junior KG curriculum establishes a strong base in these key areas.

🔢

Numeracy

  • Number recognition & writing
  • Number names & sequencing
  • Basic addition (oral)
  • Compare: more/less, before/after
🔤

Literacy

  • Caps & Small letters writing
  • Phonics sounds & Blending
  • Three-letter words
  • Simple sentences (oral)
🔊

Phonics

  • Sound recognition
  • Beginning & ending sounds
  • Picture-word association
🇮🇳

Hindi

  • Alphabet recognition
  • Writing basic letters
  • Simple Hindi words
  • Oral practice
🌍

General Awareness

  • Community helpers
  • Good habits & manners
  • Festivals & Environment
  • Personal information
🎨

Skills & Arts

  • Rhymes & Action songs
  • Colouring & Drawing
  • Recitation & Crafts
